# Business Plan — SwarmBrowser

## Product

Headless browser API — render, screenshot, scrape, and generate PDFs from 4 continents.

## Use Cases

1. **Screenshot API** — Generate screenshots of any URL for previews, social cards, etc.
2. **PDF generation** — Convert web pages to PDF (invoices, reports, documentation)
3. **Web scraping** — Extract structured data from rendered JavaScript-heavy pages
4. **SEO auditing** — Check how pages render from different geographic locations
5. **Competitive monitoring** — Track competitor pricing, content changes
6. **Compliance** — Verify site accessibility worldwide

## Market

Headless browser APIs are a growing market:
- **Browserless** — $40/mo
- **ScrapingBee** — $49/mo
- **Apify** — $49/mo

## Differentiation

1. **4 continents** — Render from any region. See how your CDN performs.
2. **Cheaper** — $20/mo for 10K pages vs $40-49/mo competitors
3. **USDC payment** — Crypto-native, no credit card
4. **Batch processing** — Submit 1000 URLs at once
5. **Geographic rendering** — Unique: choose which continent renders

## Pricing

| Tier | Pages/day | Locations | Price |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Free | 100 | 1 | $0/mo |

- Monthly subscription in USDC
- 95% to operator, 5% Opsalis settlement
- Overage billing at per-page rate
